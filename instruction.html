HOW TO PLAY



Instructions for Hunt the virus: You are presented with a board of squares. Some squares contain viruses, others don't. If you click on a square containing a virus, you lose. If you manage to click all the squares (without clicking on any viruses) you win. Clicking a square which doesnt have a virus reveals the number of neighbouring squares containing viruses. Use this information plus some guess work to avoid the viruses. To open a square, point at the square and click on it.
Detailed Instructions: A squares (neighbours) are the squares adjacent above, below, left, right, and all 4 diagonals. Squares on the sides of the board or in a corner have fewer neighbors. The board does not wrap around the edges. If you open a square with 0 neighboring viruses, all its neighbors will automatically open. This can cause a large area to automatically open.
